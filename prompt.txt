Evaluate retirement eligibility for a person based on deterministic policy rules.

Input:
A Person record with these fields:
- age: integer
- years_of_contributions: integer
- disability_status: boolean
- hazardous_job_years: integer

Decision policy:
A person is considered eligible for retirement if at least one of the following rules is satisfied:

Rule A: Standard retirement
- The person's age is at least 67
- The person has at least 10 years of contributions

Rule B: Early retirement
- The person's age is at least 63
- The person has at least 40 years of contributions

Rule C: Disability retirement
- The person's disability_status is true
- The person has at least 5 years of contributions

Rule D: Hazardous occupation retirement
- The person's age is at least 60
- The person has worked at least 20 years in hazardous occupations

Invalid input rules:
- age must be 0 or greater
- years_of_contributions must be 0 or greater
- hazardous_job_years must be 0 or greater

Output:
Return true only if at least one retirement rule is satisfied.
Return false if none of the rules is satisfied.
Do not use subjective judgment, external knowledge, or assumptions beyond the provided fields.
